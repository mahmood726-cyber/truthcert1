SUMMARY OF THE WORK

Meta-analysis is fundamental to evidence-based medicine, yet existing tools either require programming expertise or lack integrated decision-support capabilities. We present TruthCert-PairwisePro, a browser-based meta-analysis platform that addresses this gap through three innovations:

1. Comprehensive statistical methods validated against R's metafor package (109 tests across 4 benchmark datasets)
2. A novel verdict classification system (STABLE/MODERATE/UNCERTAIN) with documented operating characteristics (Type I error 3.1%, sensitivity 70.3%)
3. Integrated health technology assessment with verdict-gated recommendations

The platform requires no installation, runs entirely in-browser, and is freely available as open-source software.
