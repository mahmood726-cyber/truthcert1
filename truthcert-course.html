The Problem We Solve
THE CHALLENGE
Health policy decisions in Africa often rely on evidence from high-income countries. But treatment effects, costs, and outcomes may not transfer directly across contexts.
OUR SOLUTION
TruthCert Africa Packs provide structured, transparent frameworks for adapting evidence to local contexts while maintaining rigorous certification standards.

Discounting Parameters
DEFAULT DISCOUNT RATE
3% per year for both costs and outcomes
SENSITIVITY ANALYSIS
Test with 0%, 3%, and 5% to assess robustness

Discounting ensures future costs and benefits are expressed in present-day terms.

Evidence Go/No-Go Rules
Minimum studies required for meta-analysis

GO (k >= 4)

Proceed with full HTA analysis

CAUTION (k = 2-3)

Proceed with explicit uncertainty

NO-GO (k = 1)

Pivot to broader disease group

BLOCK (k = 0)

Cannot certify any claims

TruthCert Certification
Four levels of evidence certainty

STABLE

Full HTA, PSA, and VOI allowed. High confidence in results.

MODERATE

Full analysis with widened uncertainty. Emphasize regret framing.

EXPOSED

Limited HTA. Scenario-bounded outputs with structural uncertainty.

UNCERTAIN

No precision allowed. Bounds-only, worst-case, value-of-research.

🔒

No Naked Numbers

Every number shown to users must come from a certified claim in a TruthCert bundle, or be explicitly labelled UNCERTIFIED.

"If you can't show the evidence locator, you can't show the number."

Memory-Leak = BLOCK
CRITICAL RULE
If a claim's evidence references memory:* or any memory-based source, certification must BLOCK.

Memory can guide planning, but it can never serve as evidence for certified claims.

WTP Threshold Policy
IMPORTANT
TruthCert does NOT assume GDP-multiple thresholds by default. This avoids well-known criticisms of arbitrary cost-effectiveness thresholds.
RECOMMENDED APPROACH
Use affordability analysis and net health benefit framing instead of fixed WTP thresholds.
